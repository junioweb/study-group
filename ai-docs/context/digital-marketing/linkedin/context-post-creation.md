## Response Context: Delivery Format Specifications
**Neuroscience-Optimized Post Structure:**
```
[NEURO-HOOK: 7-10 word pattern interrupt that creates unresolved tension]
[Example: "I fired my best client last Tuesday." OR "This mistake cost me $87,000."]

[STORY SETUP: 1-2 sentences establishing emotional stakes with sensory detail]
[Example: "My stomach dropped when I saw the email notification at 2:37 AM."]

[BRAIN-BRIDGE: 1 sentence connecting personal story to universal professional principle]
[Example: "What I learned about setting boundaries applies to every service business."]

[VALUE DELIVERY: 2-3 short paragraphs with specific frameworks/data, maintaining mini-cliffhangers]
[Include 1 dopamine trigger: "Most people miss the third element that changes everything..."]

[VULNERABILITY PEAK: 1 sentence with specific personal cost/lesson - oxytocin trigger]
[Example: "I lost three nights of sleep questioning my entire career choice before realizing..."]

[CONVERSATION CATALYST: Open-ended question inviting personal professional experience]
[Example: "When have you had to choose between your values and your revenue? What happened?"]

[LINE BREAK]

[NEURO-HASHTAGS: 3-5 tags mixing emotional + professional triggers]
[Example: #VulnerableLeadership #BusinessBoundaries #StartupLessons]
```

[ ] **Dopamine Hook Score:** Creates immediate curiosity gap requiring resolution
[ ] **Oxytocin Vulnerability Marker:** Contains specific personal failure/emotional cost (not generic advice)
[ ] **Mirror Neuron Activation:** Includes sensory details or emotional states readers can embody
[ ] **Tension-Reward Cycle:** Maintains at least 2 unresolved elements until conclusion
[ ] **Comment Trigger Design:** Question requires personal professional experience sharing
[ ] **Neuro-Visual Alignment:** Visual recommendation specifies emotional tone and neurochemical target
[ ] **Dopamine Link Strategy:** External resources positioned as "reward completion" in first comment

**Neuroscience Formatting Specifications:**
- **Paragraph Rhythm:** 1-2-1-2 sentence pattern (mimics conversational dopamine flow)
- **Sentence Length Variation:** Alternate between short punchy sentences (dopamine spikes) and longer emotional sentences (oxytocin flow)
- **Strategic Bold Emphasis:** Maximum 2 bolded phrases highlighting emotional turning points (not just key insights)
- **Visual Neuro-Guidance:** Specify image/video emotional tone (e.g., "authentic 'in the moment' photo showing frustration/relief transition")
- **Comment Response Template:** Acknowledge emotion → add value → invite deeper reflection
